Colon hydrotherapy (also called colonic irrigation or a colonic) is a safe, effective method of removing waste from the large intestine – without the use of drugs. Using regulated amounts of warm purified water, the colon is gently filled and emptied multiple times. The process softens and loosens built-up faecal matter, gas, and toxins, which are then flushed away. It rehabilitates the colon and helps reactivate its natural movement.

When performed by a qualified, certified therapist using sterile, single-use equipment, colon hydrotherapy is a safe and well-tolerated procedure. At Colon H2O, Jeanette uses sterile disposable equipment for every session – nothing is ever reused. Water pressure and temperature are carefully regulated throughout the treatment, and Jeanette is present throughout to ensure your comfort and safety. As with any health procedure, there are some contraindications – Jeanette will review your health history before treatment.

Each session takes approximately 30 to 45 minutes to complete. For first-time clients, Jeanette begins with a thorough consultation to review your health history and answer any questions, so please allow approximately 1.5 hours for your first visit. Follow-up sessions typically take less overall time as the consultation portion is shorter. Aftercare guidance is provided at the end of every session.

Before: Avoid gas-forming foods (beans, cabbage, carbonated drinks) the day before and the day of your session. Do not eat for at least 2 hours beforehand. Favour vegetables and grains, and avoid fatty foods. Bring a clean boxer short to wear during the session.

After: Your body may feel weary – this is normal. Rest where possible. Follow a basic, easy-to-digest diet as advised by Jeanette. Use the recommended intestinal flora supplements. Avoid alcohol and spicy food for at least 24 hours and drink plenty of water to support continued cleansing.
