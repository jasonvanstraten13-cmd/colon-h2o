A decade of healing from the inside out.

I'm Jeanette van der Merwe – a registered, RICTAT-certified colon hydrotherapist based at Silver Lakes Gold Estate in Pretoria. For over a decade I have helped hundreds of patients restore gut health, relieve chronic digestive discomfort, and rediscover what it feels like to feel truly well.

My Journey

The Story Behind Colon H2O

Every calling has a beginning. Here is mine – five chapters that shaped the practice and the person behind it.

Chapter 1

The Beginning

My own health journey started it all. Struggling for years with chronic bloating, fatigue, and a digestive system that simply would not cooperate, I tried every conventional route available. When I finally discovered the transformative effect of colon hydrotherapy, I knew I had found something worth dedicating my life to – not just for myself, but for the countless others quietly suffering the same way.

Chapter 2

A New Chapter

I enrolled in formal training and earned my RICTAT certification – the internationally recognised standard for colon hydrotherapy practitioners. Rigorous, demanding, and deeply rewarding, the training gave me both the clinical knowledge and the deep respect for the body that underpins everything I do at Colon H2O today.

Chapter 3

Ten Years Growing

Over the next decade, I built a busy, trusted practice in Pretoria. Session by session, patient by patient, I refined my technique and deepened my understanding of the gut–health connection. I saw first–hand how a healthy colon could change someone's energy, mood, skin, and overall quality of life – and that kept me inspired every single day.

Chapter 4

The Spark

A pivotal moment came when I started incorporating therapeutic probiotic implants at the end of each session – a practice that dramatically amplified results for my clients. Seeing patients leave not just cleansed but actively repopulating their microbiome with beneficial flora confirmed what I had long believed: true gut health requires both cleansing and restoration.

Chapter 5

The Turning Point

Colon H2O was born – a dedicated, purpose-built practice at Silver Lakes Gold Estate in Pretoria. With a fully sterile, medically equipped treatment room, a curated range of gut-health supplements, and a philosophy rooted in compassionate, client-centred care, Colon H2O became the practice I had always envisioned – one where every person who walks through the door feels heard, respected, and genuinely helped.

"I believe the colon is the cornerstone of health. When your gut functions well, everything else – your energy, your mood, your skin – follows. My purpose is to help you get there."

– Jeanette van der Merwe, Colon H2O

Credentials

RICTAT Certified

Sterile, Disposable Equipment

Every session uses single-use, sterile, FDA-cleared disposable specula and tubing – zero risk of cross-contamination, every time.

Your Experience

What to Expect at Colon H2O

From the moment you arrive at Silver Lakes, every step is designed to put you at ease and deliver the best possible result.

1
Warm Welcome & Consultation We begin with a relaxed, confidential consultation to discuss your health history, current symptoms, and goals for the session.
2
Comfortable Setup You are gently prepared in a private, warm treatment room using fully sterile, disposable equipment – your comfort at every step is our priority.
3
