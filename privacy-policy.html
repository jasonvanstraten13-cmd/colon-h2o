Your Data & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Colon H2O is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with South African law.

Effective Date: 1 January 2025  |  Last Updated: 1 January 2025

1. Introduction

Colon H2O (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the website colonh2o.co.za and provides professional colon hydrotherapy services at Silver Lakes Golf Estate, Pretoria. We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

This policy is drafted in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of the Republic of South Africa.

2. Who We Are

Responsible Party: Colon H2O
Contact Person: Jeanette van der Merwe
Address: 9 Nicklaus Street, Silver Lakes Golf Estate, Pretoria, 0081
Email: jeanette@colonh2o.co.za
Phone: 082 564 2526

3. Personal Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact details: Name, email address, phone number
  • Communication content: Messages submitted via our contact form or WhatsApp
  • Health information: Relevant medical or health history discussed in consultations (required to provide safe treatment)
  • Appointment details: Scheduled appointment times and treatment records
  • Website usage data: Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited (collected automatically)

4. How We Collect Personal Information

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • When you complete and submit our contact or booking form on this website
  • When you contact us via WhatsApp, telephone, or email
  • When you attend a consultation or treatment session at our premises
  • Automatically through standard website technical data (e.g., cookies and server logs)

5. Purpose of Processing

We collect and use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and booking requests
  • To schedule and manage your treatment appointments
  • To provide safe and appropriate colon hydrotherapy treatment (which requires relevant health information)
  • To send appointment reminders or follow-up communications (where consented)
  • To comply with our legal and professional obligations
  • To improve the functionality and content of our website

6. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information on the following legal grounds under POPIA:

  • Consent: You consent to processing by submitting your information via our website form or contacting us directly
  • Contractual necessity: Processing is necessary to book and carry out your treatment
  • Legitimate interest: We have a legitimate interest in maintaining basic contact and appointment records
  • Legal compliance: We may process information as required by applicable laws

7. Contact Form & Third-Party Processing

Our contact and booking form is powered by Formspark (formspark.io). When you submit the form, your information is transmitted to and processed by Formspark in accordance with their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Formspark’s privacy policy at formspark.io/legal/privacy-policy. Colon H2O receives your submission details via email notification.

8. Health Information

Health information is a special category of personal information under POPIA and is afforded heightened protection. Any health-related information you share with Jeanette – either via our website form or in person – is collected solely for the purpose of providing safe and appropriate treatment. This information will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law.

9. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website (e.g., Formspark for form submissions) – only what is necessary for them to perform their function
  • If required by law or a valid court order
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Colon H2O, our clients, or the public

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Contact and appointment records are generally retained for a period of up to 3 years. Health consultation records may be retained for longer periods as required by professional and legal obligations. After the retention period, personal information is securely deleted or anonymised.

11. Security of Personal Information

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes the use of secure forms, encrypted email communications where possible, and limiting access to personal information to authorised personnel only. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies – small text files placed on your device – to improve your browsing experience. We may also collect standard analytics data about how visitors use our website (pages visited, time on site, device type). This data is collected in aggregate and is not used to identify individual visitors. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may affect certain website functionality.

13. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your information in certain circumstances
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time
  • Right to complain: Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via email at jeanette@colonh2o.co.za.

14. Information Regulator

If you are not satisfied with how we handle your personal information, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa:
Website: www.inforegulator.org.za
Email: inforeg@justice.gov.za

15. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as Google Maps). These websites operate independently and have their own privacy policies. We have no control over their content or data practices and accept no responsibility for them. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

16. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete the information.

17. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Any material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
