Before & After Your Session

Getting the most from your treatment starts with a little preparation. Here is what Jeanette recommends.

Before Your Session

Avoid gas-forming foods (beans, cabbage) the day before and day of your session. Eat nothing at least 2 hours beforehand. Favour vegetables and whole grains — avoid fatty or processed food.

After Your Session

Rest is important — your body may feel weary, which is normal. Follow Jeanette’s dietary advice, use the recommended flora products, avoid alcohol and spicy food for 24 hours, and hydrate well.

How Many Sessions?

This depends on your personal goals. One colonic removes some stagnant waste; subsequent sessions remove progressively more. Many clients benefit from a short series for optimal long-term results.
